🕌 Privacy Policy

Prayer Times Plus

Last Updated: May 10, 2026

Welcome to Prayer Times Plus. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app works and what information is stored.

Important: Prayer Times Plus is designed with privacy in mind. All your data is stored locally on your device.

1. Information Storage

All your personal data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Your selected city for prayer times
  • App preferences and settings
  • Prayer tracking records and streaks
  • Downloaded Quran surahs and audio files
  • Tasbih counter history
  • Adhkar favorites

2. Permissions We Request

Location Permission

  • Why: To calculate accurate Qibla direction and find nearby mosques
  • When: Only when you use the Qibla compass or mosque finder features
  • Control: You can deny or revoke this permission anytime in your device settings

Notification Permission

  • Why: To send prayer time reminders
  • When: When you enable notifications in app settings
  • Control: You can disable notifications anytime

Storage Permission

  • Why: To save downloaded Quran audio files for offline access
  • When: When you download Quran surahs
  • Control: Required for offline Quran access

3. What We Don't Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or share:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Financial or payment information
  • Your contacts or photos
  • Your browsing history
  • Data from other apps
  • Your location history

4. Children's Privacy

Prayer Times Plus is suitable for users of all ages, including children. We do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is designed to be family-friendly and contains only Islamic educational content.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Data Control

  • View Data: All data is stored locally and accessible through app settings
  • Delete Data: Clear app data through device settings or uninstall the app
  • No Account Required: The app works without creating an account

Opt-Out Options

  • Notifications: Disable in app settings or device settings
  • Location: Deny location permission in device settings

6. Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device until you:

  • Clear the app data through device settings
  • Uninstall the app

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

8. Islamic Values

As an Islamic app, we are committed to upholding Islamic values:

  • Honesty: We are transparent about how the app works
  • Trust: We protect your privacy as a sacred trust
  • Privacy: We respect your privacy as an Islamic right

9. Disclaimer

Religious Content Disclaimer:

While we strive for accuracy in all Islamic content, including prayer times and Quran translations, we recommend verifying important religious matters with qualified Islamic scholars.

Prayer times are calculated using standard astronomical methods but may vary slightly. For critical matters, consult your local mosque or religious authority.
