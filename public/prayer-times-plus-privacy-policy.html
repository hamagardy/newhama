Last Updated: May 10, 2026

Welcome to Prayer Times Plus ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our Islamic companion app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Important: By using Prayer Times Plus, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Location Preferences: Your selected city for accurate prayer times (Erbil, Sulaymaniyah, Duhok, Kirkuk, Halabja)
  • App Settings: Your preferences for notifications, language selection, and app customization
  • User Feedback: Suggestions and feedback you submit through the app
  • Prayer Tracking: Your daily prayer completion records stored locally on your device
  • Quran Progress: Downloaded surahs and reading progress stored locally

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, time spent in app, interaction patterns
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates when using Qibla compass or mosque finder (only when permission granted)
  • Notification Tokens: Push notification tokens for sending prayer time reminders

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Financial or payment information
  • Contacts or photos from your device
  • Browsing history or data from other apps

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 Core App Functionality

  • Provide accurate prayer times based on your selected city
  • Calculate Qibla direction using GPS coordinates
  • Send prayer time notifications and reminders
  • Track your prayer completion and streaks locally
  • Display Quran with translation and audio
  • Calculate Zakat based on current gold rates
  • Track Ramadan fasting and worship activities

2.2 App Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user needs
  • Optimize app performance

2.3 Communication

  • Send important app updates and announcements
  • Respond to user feedback and suggestions
  • Notify about new features and improvements

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Prayer tracking records and streaks
  • Downloaded Quran surahs and audio files
  • App preferences and settings
  • Tasbih counter history
  • Adhkar favorites

3.2 Cloud Storage

We use Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) to store:

  • Device registration tokens for notifications
  • Anonymous usage statistics
  • User feedback and suggestions
  • App version information

3.3 Security Measures

  • Data encryption in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Secure Firebase database with access rules
  • No storage of sensitive personal information
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Minimal data collection principle

  • Purpose: Push notifications, analytics, database

5. Permissions We Request

5.1 Location Permission

  • Why: Calculate accurate Qibla direction and find nearby mosques
  • When: Only when you use Qibla compass or mosque finder
  • Control: You can deny or revoke this permission anytime

5.2 Notification Permission

  • Why: Send prayer time reminders and important updates
  • When: When you enable notifications in settings
  • Control: You can disable notifications anytime

5.3 Storage Permission

  • Why: Save downloaded Quran audio files and share prayer images
  • When: When you download Quran or share content
  • Control: Required for offline Quran access

6. Children's Privacy

Prayer Times Plus is suitable for users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed to be family-friendly and contains only Islamic educational content.

Parents and guardians are encouraged to monitor their children's use of the app. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

7.1 Access and Control

  • View Data: Most data is stored locally and accessible through app settings
  • Delete Data: Clear app data through device settings or uninstall the app
  • Export Data: Share your prayer tracking data as an image

7.2 Opt-Out Options

  • Notifications: Disable in app settings or device settings
  • Location: Deny location permission in device settings
  • Analytics: Limited tracking can be disabled in app settings

7.3 Data Deletion

To delete all your data:

  1. Uninstall the Prayer Times Plus app from your device
  2. Contact us at admin@hamagardy.com to request deletion of cloud data
  3. We will delete your data within 30 days of your request

8. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored until you clear app data or uninstall
  • Cloud Data: Retained for up to 2 years or until deletion requested
  • Analytics: Aggregated data retained indefinitely (anonymized)
  • Feedback: Stored until addressed, then archived

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country. We use Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) which has data centers worldwide. By using our app, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws.

We ensure that all data transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and use appropriate safeguards.
